A Bit About Me

Hi, my name is Jess and I am studying Computer Science at Sheffield Hallam University. I have just begun my second year of study and hope to complete a placement year before graduating in the summer of 2023.

Outside of my degree, I love to travel, as well as taking care of my many houseplants! I took time out of education and spent six months travelling solo around Australia. I would love to see more of the world in future, both in and outside of my work.

What I'm Studying

During my first year I had the opportunity to cover a broad range of subject areas, including modules in mathematics, computer architecture, and professional development, in addition to learning languages such as C++ and C#.
Since moving into second year, I have been introduced to both Java and C++ Object Oriented Programming. There is also the opportunity for extensive group work in the coming year, so I am looking forward to gaining experience coding in groups and working as team.

My current aim is to try and diversify my areas of study and gain as much experience as possible, before deciding which route I would like to take regarding my career.
